Again, it goes back to taking control of the situation rather than 
letting some "automagic" happen.

Setup your own mount point point and use the script snippets that Marcus 
has already posted. Once you have the script in place make sure you 
disable all the automount settings in your desktop environment. Then it 
will always work the same, for every USB stick you produce and on 
whatever machine you choose to boot. There's a reason for the "plug and 
pray" nickname ;)
