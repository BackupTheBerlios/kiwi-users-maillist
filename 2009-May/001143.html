Jose,

You can add packages in config.xml in the <packages> element, take a
look at the cookbook at http://en.opensuse.org/Build_Service/KIWI/Cookbook

To add cracklib-dict-full you would need to add

<package name="cracklib-dict-full"/>

HTH
Robert
