I noticed that when I boot my thin client, it is always waiting to find
tftp.$mydomain . From the kiwi.pdf I understood that it will not look for
this address when kiwiftp is given as a kernel parameter. My entry in 
/srv/tftpboot/pxelinux.cfg/default looks like this:

        kernel boot/linux
        append initrd=boot/initrd vga=normal ramdisk_size=512000

That would mean it doesn't have to wait for the tftp.$mydomain right? I am
asking this because I have two DHCP servers, and I want the LTSP one only

find tftp.$mydomain).
