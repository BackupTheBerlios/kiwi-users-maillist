> What is the WLAN chipset?

It's a RaLink WLAN chipset RT2700.
Download of the source are available under
http://www.ralinktech.com/ralink/Home/Support/Linux.html

I use this source:
http://www.ralinktech.com.tw/data/drivers/2008_0918_RT2860_Linux_STA_v1.8.0.0.tar.bz2

It support 802.11 b/g/n WLAN connection with WEP/WPA1/WPA2.
