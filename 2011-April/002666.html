We had this conversation previously, thus just a few hints: If you look 
at the content of 
http://download.opensuse.org/repositories/filesystems/openSUSE_11.3/x86_64/ 
you will see that the clicfs package changed today, and now things are 
broken on your end.

Again, if you depend on packages in development repositories this is 
stuff you have to expect and you have to be able to handle. The only way 
to avoid such issues is to stick with the "official" project repositories

- http://download.opensuse.org/distribution/11.3/repo/oss/
- http://download.opensuse.org/distribution/11.3/repo/non-oss/
- http://download.opensuse.org/update/11.3/

These may not provide the functionality you need, but these are the only 
repositories pretty much guaranteed to be stable.
