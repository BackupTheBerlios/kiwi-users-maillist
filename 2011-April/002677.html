On 04/21/2011 04:35 PM, Josh Duvall wrote:
> My name is Josh and I am building an appliance with KIWI. I cannot seem
> to figure out how to incorporate LVM group.
>
> When I add:
> {code}
>
> <systemdisk name="fismaVG">
> <volume name="var" size="4000M"/>
> </systmedisk>
>
> {code}
>
> ... To the preferences section, I keep getting this error:
>
> Element preferences has extra content: systemdisk
> Relaz-NG validity error: extra element preferences in interleave
>
> .....failed to validate ect.........
>
> In the CookBook on pg. 15 it says that the system disk goes in the
> preferences element.

Sorry the documentation is incorrect. I will fix it and submit a patch, 
should be included in next release.

The <systemdisk> element is a child of the <type> element, i.e. you 
should have
<type .....>
     <systemdisk ...>
         ......
     </systemdisk>
</type>

If you have the kiwi-doc package installed you can take a look at the 
schema requirements by pointing your browser to:

file:///usr/share/doc/packages/kiwi/schema/kiwi.html

>
> I have used lvm="true" in the <type> line.  do i also add lvmgroup=??????

Please take a look at the docs specific to the version of Kiwi you are 
using, lvm="true" for example no longer exists in the latest release 
(4.87). I forget what lvmgroup used to be, sorry.
