Hi,

> Where can I find, or how can I extract, a list of openSUSE patterns
> suitable for use in a kiwi config.xml file?  I thought I saw some kind
> of "pattern*.xml" downloaded during autorefresh when I launched the
> yast2 installer, but I haven't been able to locate it again on
> download.opensuse.org.
> 
> I've read through http://en.opensuse.org/Patterns, but while the
> pattern names are obvious for pre-11.1, it's not obvious what the
> machine-readable name for patterns are for 11.1.
> 
> I'm particularly interested in 11.1 or 11.2.

You can try to do this:

  rm /var/cache/kiwi/satsolver/*
  kiwi --list-xmlinfo /usr/share/kiwi/image/isoboot/suse-11.1 \
       --set-repo /path/to/11.1/repo

  *** ignore the output ***

  dumpsolv /var/cache/kiwi/satsolver/* | grep pattern:

the kiwi --list-xmlinfo function creates a solvable from the
given repository which also contains the pattern information.
The dumpsolv tool just dumps the data in a human readable
format and gives you a list of all patterns in that solvable
respectively in that distro
