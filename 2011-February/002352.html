Yes, there are technically two images, one is a special live system that
knows how to dump the image intended for installation onto the target
device. In config.xml you configure the image intended for installation.
The config.xml for all intend and purposes has no influence on the
"special live image" (I am glossing over some details here to simplify
things a little bit).

Therefore as a creator of an OEM image you do not have to worry about
the "image" that does the installation. Focus on the image that you want
installed, as that's the image you are configuring in your config tree
(config.xml, config.sh, images.sh, and overlay-tree).
