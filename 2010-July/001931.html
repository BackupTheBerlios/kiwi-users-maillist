If I were you I would create a minimal system with all the common packages of your users and then, on first boot, you open a firefox browser with an html page .

This html page should be local and have links to "one click installs". "one click installs" let's you add and remove packages, as well as add repositories.

html pages are easier to write than yast modules and, with a good designer, can be really nice.

just an idea that may simplify your problem
