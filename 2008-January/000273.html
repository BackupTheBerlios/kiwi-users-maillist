Hi,

> > So you don't use NFS root at the moment but the "standard" atftp
> > based deployment method correct ? In this case you should first
> > have a look into your atftpd configuration. You should disable
> > multicast at all in a first try:
> > 
> > 	vi /etc/sysconfig/atftpd
> > 	ATFTPD_OPTIONS="--daemon --no-multicast"
> > 
> > restart atftpd, try again and run
> > 
> > 	tail -f /var/log/messages
> > 
> > while the client "unpack/download" the image
> 
> 
> Ok,
> 
> after recovering the system (dhcp was broken) and installing atftp I
> made a test with --no-multicast.
> The problem is still the same. Unpacking starts and stops after some
> seconds.

very strange but now you can have a look at the log file:

	/var/log/messages

why it stops should be listed here. Additionally you can add
the option --verbose in /etc/sysconfig/atftp and restart atftp
you will get more information in this case
