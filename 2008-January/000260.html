> Normally only the imagefile is stored in the directory and Kiwi must
> unpack these files.
> The problem:
> Unpacking stops after some seconds and I ve no idea why.

So you don't use NFS root at the moment but the "standard" atftp
based deployment method correct ? In this case you should first
have a look into your atftpd configuration. You should disable
multicast at all in a first try:

	vi /etc/sysconfig/atftpd
	ATFTPD_OPTIONS="--daemon --no-multicast"

restart atftpd, try again and run

	tail -f /var/log/messages

while the client "unpack/download" the image
