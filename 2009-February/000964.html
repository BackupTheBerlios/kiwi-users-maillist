> How can I manage that with kiwi ?. just for the mkinitrd -s

I already sent you an e-mail about custom branding for grub and
the splash screen: You need your own copy of the kiwi boot image

---snip---

cp -a /usr/share/kiwi/image/oemboot/suse-SLED10 \
   /usr/share/kiwi/image/oemboot/suse-SLED10-Thierry

cd /usr/share/kiwi/image/oemboot/suse-SLED10-Thierry
rm .checksum.md5
mkdir -p root/etc/bootsplash/themes/SuSE-NLD/images

Now check the contents of the package bootsplash-theme-SuSE-NLD with rpm
you can do that by calling "rpm -qpl
/path/to/your/SLED/repo/suse/i586/bootsplash-theme-SuSE-NLD". You will see many
picture and logo files in the SuSE-NLD/images directory. If you replace by exact
names the one you want to adapt
according to your needs and copy it to the directory created above you will
get your own branding in grub and as part of the bootsplash

in your config.xml file you need to change the boot attribute to point
to your 

   boot="oemboot/suse-SLED10-Thierry"

boot image description and after you
call the create step again you should see your own branded version

---snap---

kiwi re-creates the initrd from your version of the boot image and
therefore includes the branded files
