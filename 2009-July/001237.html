> Why does the kiwi-templates package depend on qemu?

kiwi-templates requires kiwi-desc-vmxboot and 
kiwi-desc-vmxboot requires qemu multipath-tools

kiwi uses the qemu-img tool that's the reason why
qemu is a requirement
