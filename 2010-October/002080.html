I was able to build a btrfs image on 11.3... hmm, can you test the
following:

   dd if=/dev/zero of=/tmp/btr bs=1M count=500
   losetup /dev/loop0 /tmp/btr
   mkfs.btrfs /dev/loop0
   
   blkid /dev/loop0  

   losetup -d /dev/loop0

You should see:

   /dev/loop0: UUID="77e46e89-cb51-45e1-ba00-fb7a7095783c" UUID_SUB="9242b801-f1ee-49c7-bc96-44cada8d6b23" TYPE="btrfs"
