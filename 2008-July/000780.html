Hello community

We had our weekly meeting on IRC #ltsp yesterday, Jan has posted the
minutes and transcript here[1].

I am happy to announce the availability of first Easy-LTSP packages
from the openSUSE Build Service repositories[2]. The packages are
available for all current versions of openSUSE, SLE10 and Fedora 9.
Thanks to Lars for packaging them.

The lts.conf editing implementation is in the initial stage, so don't
expect it to work just yet, you will however get a good idea about
where the GUI is heading.

As we do not have anyone with debian packaging skills packages for
Debian and Ubuntu are not available yet, users of other distributions
can however download the tarball[3] containing prebuilt binaries, the
GUI can be launched by running "mono Easy-LTSP.exe" in the folder
where tarball is extracted. I'd be happy to give access to server:ltsp
repo to anyone who wants to help with deb packaging.

KIWI-LTSP would be going to OSCON 08[4], so if you are in that part of
the world, do check it out.

As always feedbacks and suggestions are most welcome.

Kind regards

-J
