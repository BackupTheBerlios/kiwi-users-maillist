It's not required. The main reason is that along with the list
you also have a profile in that group which you can only manage
if you have an account. Without an account you can be added via
an invitation. Would you like me to send you one ?

> If the idea is to stay neutral to all

I agree it shouldn't be required to open a new account just to
follow the mailing list. The kiwi group is an open group so you
also can subscribe to the group by sending an e-mail to:

  kiwi-images+subscribe at googlegroups.com

Read more about what you can do on the group with and without an
account here:

  http://www.google.com/support/groups/bin/answer.py?hl=en&topic=9257&answer=46438
