Yes, but I think you need a gmail account to make this work.

1. You will need to add your suse.de e-mail to your gmail account as an alternative e-mail.
2. Then you subscribe to the group.
3. Then go to groups.google.com and go to "Manage my memberships"
4. Select your alternative account for any of the groups that you have subscribed for.
