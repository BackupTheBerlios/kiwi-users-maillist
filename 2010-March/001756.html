See /usr/share/kiwi/images/netboot/suse-*

Check out /usr/share/doc/packages/kiwi/kiwi.pdf from kiwi-docs package.

You will have to create profile for your custom flavored kernel, add
it to the config.xml of netboot image as well as system image,
/usr/share/kiwi/images/ltsp/suse-* in your case. Change --add-profile
yourcustomprofile in
/usr/share/kiwi/image/ltsp/include/kiwi-ltsp-functions.sh

Kiwi's initrd and kernels are in /srv/tftpboot/boot/ to create those
images use kiwi-ltsp-setup -n after adapting netboot image
description.

This is more of a kiwi question so copying it to
kiwi-users at lists.berlios.de, you can post any further queries on it as
more expert knowledge would be available there.
