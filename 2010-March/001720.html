> ---------------------------------------------------
> Oct-27 04:10:32 <3> : Integrity check for
> /usr/share/kiwi/image/isoboot/suse-11.2 failed:
> ./config.xml: FAILED
> ./images.sh: OK

> ./root/linuxrc: OK
> ./root/etc/modprobe.conf.local: OK
> ./root/preinit: OK
> md5sum: WARNING: 1 of 6 computed checksums did NOT match   failed
> ---------------------------------------------------

This means you have changed the config.xml of a boot image (initrd)

   /usr/share/kiwi/image/isoboot/suse-11.2/config.xml

Why did you do that ? Because the initrd (boot image descriptions) are
very important for booting the system I added a checksum to detect
if anybody changes what has been delivered via the packages. 

> What should I do to should this problem?

re-install the package kiwi-desc-isoboot
