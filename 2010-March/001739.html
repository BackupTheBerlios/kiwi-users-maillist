Yeah, I did warn you. :-)

Anyway, there is another way. Fairly recent (10 years or so) BIOSes provide
a 'boot next device' function which normally does what you want in this
situation.

I've done a quick check and it worked on the two test machines I tried and
in vmware. No luck in qemu and vbox (which is based on the same bios code,
iirc).

So, if you can live with that limitation, we can try this.
