Needs to look as follows;

<repository type="yast2">
   <source path="opensuse://11.4/repo/oss/"/>
</repository>
<repository type="rpm-md">
   <source path="opensuse://11.4/repo/update/"/>
</repository>


BTW, some of the examples that are in the kiwi-doc package show how to 
specify multiple repositories.
