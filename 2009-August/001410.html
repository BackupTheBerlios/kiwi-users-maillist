Hi,

just to let you know I have adapted the oem- bool
elements from content "text" to boolean. This means
only "true" or "false" are allowed as values. If you
have something like yes or no you need to adapt it

The schema will not validate if you don't adapt it
but the error message from libXML doesn't directly
point someone to this problem

That's the reason why I'm writing it on the list
