To answer my own question, and for the record.

Based on what I have learned this appears to be strictly an issue with 
running 32 bit apps on a 64 bit system. On SLE 10 based systems this can 
be reproduced with Firefox. Installing glibc-locale-32bit and gucharmap 
packages makes the problem go away.
