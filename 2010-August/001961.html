Oh, right my fault. I created the patch from the current
master code. Just do the following:

- open /usr/share/kiwi/modules/KIWIImage.pm
- search for

     sub createImageSplit

  search for

     $main::ForeignRepo{"xmlnode"} = $xml -> getForeignNodeList();

  and add the following line after it:

     $main::ForeignRepo{"xmlobj"}  = $xml;

The patch is a one liner so not too difficult :)
