On Tue, Jun 15, 2010 at 3:54 PM, Robert Schweikert
<rschweikert at novell.com> wrote:
>
> I tried getting the kiwi boot log (kiwidebug=1), but couldn't figure out
> how to get anything persistent attached to the qemu VM. I tried using
>
> qemu-kvm ..... -drive file=/dev/sdb1,index=1,media=disk
>
> with /dev/sdb1 being a usb stick on the system I am testing, but the
> device didn't show up in the qemu VM.
>

This is what I use:


qemu-kvm -m 1024M -monitor stdio -boot d -cdrom imagename.iso -serial
file:/tmp/kiwilog

Once booted, run this in the image: cat /var/log/boot.kiwi > /dev/ttyS0
