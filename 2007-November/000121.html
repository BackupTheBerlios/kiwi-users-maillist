Hi,

> Question:
> Is it possible to get aufs/unionfs support in the kernel for images
> based on SLE10 SP1?  Without building a custom kernel?

I'm sorry this will not happen because aufs and unionfs are both
"problematic" to become part of the kernel, especially the SP1 kernel.
We are currently fighting to still have a kernel package available for
our current kernel.

Problem is the kernel people don't like the implementation of these
filesystems. They see serios problems in different areas of the code.
As far as I understand they are right with their concerns but 
on the other hand nobody is willing to fix the code to make it 
a clean filesystem.

I think aufs or unionfs is a great tool which can be used in many
places very easily. One can have real read-only mounts as well as
combining filesystems of different layers into one. I'm really
wondering why we (Novell) don't push that technology. So I'm sorry
but that stuff will not be available for SLE products now

I will continue to fighting but I can't promise a success here

I'm really sorry
