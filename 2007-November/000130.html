I have similar problems and still no clue what happened. I contacted
our experts on this maybe you want to do the same. They need some
information in order to check what happened:

	- network/config:DEBUG=yes
		-> creates information in syslog or on the console.
	- in /sbin/ifup{,-dhcp} and network/scripts/ifplugd-selectif there is
	  a line

			# . scripts/extradebug

      please activate it. That creates logfiles /tmp/exdeb*

1) rcnetwork stop
2) add all debug options described above
3) rcnetwork start -o boot

After that contact Christian Zoz <zoz at novell.com> or create a bug
report on bugzilla.novell.com assigned to him.
