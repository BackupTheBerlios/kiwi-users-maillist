Hi,

> If I issue "df" or other filesystem usage commands, only udev (usage 1%,
> 1 GByte) is shown there, no /home, no /tmp, no /var.
> How can I find out about current filesystem usage (e.g. the persistent
> partition) and Ramdisk usage?
> In the Desktop, I can see the ext3 volume for persistence plus the
> squashfs volume on the stick, but no usage.

Try this:

   cat /proc/mount > /etc/mtab
   df -h
