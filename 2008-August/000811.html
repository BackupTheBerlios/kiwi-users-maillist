By KDE Base system you mean KDE3 or KDE4? There are both patterns.

>  <packages patternPackageType="onlyRequired" type="image">

Leave this as is.

> or maybe I could use some pattern with same configuration ?

Add <opensusePattern name="kde3_basis"/> and/or <opensusePattern 
name="kde4_basis"/> into <packages>. Eg:

  <packages patternPackageType="onlyRequired" type="image">
    <opensusePattern name="kde3_basis"/>
