Thanks for your response.

I looked around and came to a conclusion that this was probably due to the missing COMBINED_IMAGE value.

I just added the following to the Exports (Configuration)  section in oemboot/suse-linuxrc file and that did the trick.

 export COMBINED_IMAGE="yes"
