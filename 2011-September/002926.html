i had an old desktop with intel 945 chipset and it would always give me a
"boot error" with syslinux, while the usb stick would work on other
computers. I also have a similar issue on a box with intel D945GCLF2 mb
which is a bit more modern hardware.

You might want to try formatting the usb device into usb-zip compatibility
mode with syslinux's mkdiskimage, installing syslinux on it, and then
copying over the files.

http://www.knoppix.net/wiki/Bootable_USB_Key#Large_USB_keys

Or you could try a usb stick of different size.
