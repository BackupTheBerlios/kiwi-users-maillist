> Dear fellows,
> 
> I'm trying to create a really small openSUSE image. I tried to use the
> opensusePattern=minimal_base but the image still have 166MB. Is it
> possible to reduce more?

I have made a set of images that boot with PXE. The three files and
their sizes are:

	kernel   3676640
	initrd  30120917
	root    38543485

If this was in one image, I would think it would be smaller as there is
some duplication.

I do not have X. But there are most basic network services, including
nfs. I have also removed some kernel drivers I do not need. 

Removing X is usually the biggest savings.
