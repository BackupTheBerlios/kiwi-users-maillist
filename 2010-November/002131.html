You can use the development packages:

http://download.opensuse.org/repositories/Virtualization:/Appliances/openSUSE_11.1/

These are packages based on the active development tree and there's a
new release almost every Friday.

The only thing that is broken in the packages you can pull today, AFAIK,
is the conversion from raw to qcow2 formats, a fix will be available
this coming Friday.

You can also use

http://download.opensuse.org/repositories/home:/ctso/openSUSE_11.1/

as a repo. The version of Kiwi here revs a bit less frequently than the
dev version. I do not know the criteria when this gets updated. Marcus
can fill in the blanks ;)
