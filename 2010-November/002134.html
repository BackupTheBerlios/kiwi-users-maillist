Hi,

> So, how would I create one volume for root and another for /home? I am a bit 
> confused since the documentation says (or I understood) that you need 
> systemdisk for LVM and it seems you can't have both oemconfig and systemdisk 
> sections (kiwi complains) in your config.xml.

systemdisk and oemconfig is valid together. The automatic conversion
via xslt should also give you a correct result. For example:

<type ...
    <oemconfig>
          <oem-systemsize>2048</oem-systemsize>
    </oemconfig>
    <systemdisk>
        <volume name="/home" freespace="200M"/>
    </systemdisk>
</type>

...must be accepted. It will hold the main volume group in a
partition of 2048MB and inside it will manage a home volume
with at least 200MB free space
