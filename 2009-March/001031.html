aufs doesn't exist on SLE11 correct. Given that you can't build an image
with the flags="unified" set in your config.xml. But you have other
choices which doesn't require aufs:

   a) flags="compressed"
   b) no flags attribute specified at all

- The first one allows you to specify in a <split> section what
  should be read-write (in RAM) and what should be read-only. The
  read-only part will be compressed using squashfs

- The second one just uses a static list of paths and make them
  read-write in a tmpfs

I have documented that in the ISO chapter of the kiwi.pdf.
Unfortunately I can't do more. Be aware that if you add software
not natively part of the SLE distribution your image will not
have the SLE status and might not be supported by anybody
