AFAIK, KIWI image building is not enabled on the Build Service's home projects 
by default (requires lots of resources). It can be enabled on a user's home 
project if there is a good reason for it. Nevertheless, you may branch kde-
four-live, build it locally with your own modifications and even submit your 
enhancements as well ;-)

The relevant commands are:
osc branch KDE:Medias kde-four-live
osc co home:yourusername:branches:KDE:Medias/kde-four-live
cd home\:yourusername\:branches\:KDE\:Medias/kde-four-live/
osc build
