Hi Eduardo,

when you are going to update KIWI using SLEPOS11-SP1, you need to use
the KIWI updates which are available for SLEPOS only. Other or newer
KIWI versions might differ, to the version which is released together
with SLEPOS11-SP1.

When updating Kiwi you also need to update the image templates and
rebuild your image.

>From my point of view and experience with the latest SLEPOS version, I
don't know anything about a bug, that the netboot image fails to format
the disks on IBM SurePOS systems.
