Following should fix it:

1) fix the repo entry, you have a repodata in your custom repo so it's rpm-md

  <repository type="rpm-md">
     <source path="/root/rpmbuild/RPMS/x86_64"/>
  </repository>


2) update repodata

   cd /root/rpmbuild/RPMS/x86_64
   createrepo .

3) fix your package entry acccording to the current name of your
   package in /root/rpmbuild/RPMS/x86_64. e.g if it's hello2

   <package name="hello2"/>
