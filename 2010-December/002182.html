Just for the records, this brought me back on track:

        https://lists.berlios.de/pipermail/kiwi-users/2010-June/001854.html

I just got hit by the "parted version mismatch" issue mentioned above.

Kiwi build env (OpenSuse 11.3):
		        
	os11-3:/usr/sbin # parted -v
        parted (GNU parted) 2.2

versus

Kiwi deployment (SLES 11 SP1):

        linux:~ # parted -v
        parted (GNU parted) 1.8.8

So basically building the ISO on another "SLES11 SP1" system did the trick.
