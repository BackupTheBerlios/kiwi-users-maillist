> -----Ursprüngliche Nachricht-----

> DHCP variables I am interested in. It is those that will be placed in
> the environment and thus be available to an init script.

I do this every day with kiwi generated images. The shell variables are available in  /var/lib/dhcpcd/dhcpcd-ethX.info. Simply call export `cat /var/lib/dhcpcd/dhcpcd-ethX.info` in your init script in order to make these variables available to the environment.
